Notarization:

You cannot select the archive of a pref pane for notarization, this is why I made an installer. I did it along these steps:
Sign the pane:
codesign -v -f -s "Developer ID Application: Your Name (ABCDEFG1234)" Hosts.prefPane

Build Package (pane in folder "build"):
pkgbuild --root build --identifier "nl.your-domain.hosts" --version "1.4.3" --install-location "/Library/PreferencePanes" --sign "Developer ID Installer: Your Name (ABCDEFG1234)" Hosts-1.4.3.pkg

To certify the installer, you need your ProviderShortname. For this, you have to give altool a password:
xcrun altool --list-providers -u "your.name@your-domain.nl" -p "@keychain:Developer-altool"

Certify package:
xcrun altool --notarize-app --primary-bundle-id "nl.your-domain.hosts" --username "your.name@your-domain.nl" --password "@keychain:Developer-altool" --asc-provider "YourName123456" --file "Hosts-1.4.3.pkg"

Wait, check status:
xcrun altool --notarization-info "YourRequestUUID" --username "your.name@your-domain.nl" --password "@keychain:Developer-altool"

Staple notarization ticket to package for offline installation:
xcrun stapler staple Hosts-1.4.3.pkg
